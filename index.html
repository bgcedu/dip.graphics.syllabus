Learning Outcome

This syllabus equips students with hands-on skills to create, edit, and enhance visual content, including branding materials, marketing graphics, digital illustrations, and user interface designs. Upon completion, students will be job-ready for careers in graphic design, digital marketing, branding, and multimedia production.
